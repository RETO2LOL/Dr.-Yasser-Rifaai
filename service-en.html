Our Pediatric Dental Services

At RAM Clinics — Khobar, Al Rakah Branch, Dr. Yasser Rifaai provides comprehensive pediatric dental care designed to ensure your child’s comfort, safety, and long-term oral health. Our services cover preventive, restorative, cosmetic, and specialized care — including treatment for children with special needs.

Cavity Treatment

Early diagnosis of cavities and the use of safe, child-friendly filling materials to preserve dental health.

Cosmetic & Restorative Fillings

High-quality materials that provide strength, durability, and a natural aesthetic for your child’s smile.

Baby Teeth Extraction

Safe and fast extraction procedures when clinically necessary, with close follow-up to protect permanent teeth.

Special Needs Dentistry

Expert care tailored to children with special needs, supported by specialized techniques and equipment.

Preventive Care & Cleaning

Fluoride applications, dental cleaning, and fissure sealants to prevent cavities and maintain healthy teeth.

Trauma & Emergency Care

Rapid and effective management of dental injuries such as fractures and accidental trauma.

General Anesthesia & Full-Mouth Treatment

Complete dental rehabilitation under general anesthesia in one session (usually under 2 hours), ensuring a stress-free experience.
