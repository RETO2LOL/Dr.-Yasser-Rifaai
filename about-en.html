Who Is Dr. Yasser Rifaai?

Dr. Yasser Rifaai is a Pediatric Dentistry Consultant with over 20 years of experience in providing specialized dental care for children. He is well-known for his gentle approach, exceptional skill in managing anxious children, and his expertise in treating children with special needs using proven behavioral and scientific methods that ensure a safe and comfortable experience.

His professional journey began in 2004, and he earned his PhD from Al-Azhar University in 2015. Dr. Yasser combines clinical excellence with academic contributions, having taught in multiple universities and published scientific research in reputable international journals.

Academic & Research Experience

Throughout his career, Dr. Yasser has built a strong academic record, including numerous peer-reviewed publications in pediatric dentistry and participation in local and international conferences. He has served as a professor and head of Pediatric Dentistry departments in various Egyptian and Saudi universities. His clinical practice is grounded in the latest evidence-based protocols to ensure children receive the highest standard of dental care.

Current Practice

Dr. Yasser currently practices at RAM Clinics — Khobar, Al Rakah Branch, where he provides comprehensive pediatric dental services including preventive care, restorative treatments, and behavior guidance for children during dental procedures.
